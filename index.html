Oh hey! Welcome to Heron, your map maker.

Click on the map to place a marker and then take a screenshot. Show or hide labels with the checkbox on the left, then click Refresh map.

You can also add terrain or satellite imagery with the buttons in the top right.

If you want to hide page furniture like the options box or zoom bar, press the h key. To clear your marker or to close this window, press esc.
